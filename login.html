About Baloch Transport Service

The Baloch Transport Bus Service is one of the largest passenger transport company of Pakistan with an enviable passenger base and track record, operating across Punjab, Sindh, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and Balochistan. Founded in 1955 by (Late) Ahmad Nawaz Khitran.
